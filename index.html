About Me - Bruno Caraffa

Since a little kid I've been extremely curious. The first dataset I ever had contact with was a world map that my dad gave me when I was 8. I memorized as many countries and the respective capitals and flags I could just for fun.

Data Science was what Inneeded to feed my constant curiosity in the adult life. During the pandemic I had the time to dive deeper into this world. Since then I've been working in projects envolving image classification, price prediction, sentiment analysis and clustering among others. I love to write Medium articles about those projects, and you can check them here.

I work with data since 2017 when I began my career in a auto retailing group. The dynamic pace of the retailing sector was the perfect environment to learn how data can be a leverage to companies. Graduated in Industrial Engineering in 2019 at the University of Brasilia and currently work as a Data Intelligence Coordinator at Wiz Co, where I lead a team of analysts to help the top management to evaluate the performance of business units with reports and predictions.
